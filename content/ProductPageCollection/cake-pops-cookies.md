#### Cookies

We make delicious, iced, vanilla bean sugar cookies that we can match to your party, theme, or color. They can also be customized to be your wedding favors with a thank you, a monogram, a wedding cake, or even a bridal gown and tuxedo! We have a large collection of assorted cookie cutters to choose from and with advanced notice, we can even custom order new ones for your order!  We also offer standard cookies such as chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, lemon shortbread, and more.

One dozen minimum per design.


#### Cake-pops

Cake-pops are a truffle on a stick, covered in dark or white chocolate. They make great small bites or a great addition to a dessert bar.  At Cake Elizabeth, we can make them in different shapes, colors, and many flavors to suit your needs!

#### Flavors & order policies

Chocolate, Vanilla, Strawberry, Red Velvet, Lemon.

One doz. minimum per flavor and design
